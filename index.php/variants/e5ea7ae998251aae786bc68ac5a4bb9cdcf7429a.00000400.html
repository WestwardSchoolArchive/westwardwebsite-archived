A Word From The Head

 

Westward is a nurturing, co-educational day School offering small classes and excellent pastoral care from dedicated, experienced teachers. Our children benefit from a first class academic education and a wealth of opportunities to explore new talents within a happy, caring and secure environment.

 

The Westward curriculum primarily focuses on developing the skills of reading, writing and mathematics, equipping children with the knowledge and learning tools they need to embark with confidence on their senior school journeys. We also place a strong emphasis on a wide curriculum with Music, Art and Drama being particular strengths.

 

We are very proud of the high academic standards our children achieve, with many children attaining scholarships every year.

 

Our ISI Report commented ‘Throughout the School, relationships between staff and pupils and among pupils themselves, are excellent.’

 

Please contact the School Office to join one of our Friday visiting mornings and experience the friendly, family atmosphere that is our School community.


 

Headteacher
