Netball V Ashton House

 

The U10 and U11 girls team travelled to Grasshoppers to play Ashton House on Friday 7th October.

 

The Ashton House U11 team had four very tall players and played very well in the first half.  Westward seemed surprised by their height and they did not settle into their usual routine. Chloe and Sienna tried hard in defence and in the second half the centre court started to flow and the speed and strength of Eirinn in the circle really helped catch up from a half-time deficit of 2-3. Leonie played very well with Eirinn and we scored some very useful goals giving us a 6-4 lead. Well done, girls! Eirinn was Player of the Match and thoroughly deserved it.

The U10s were never in any serious danger; Sarah was in full control of the shooting circle and despite playing in a full height post, they pulled ahead in the first half. Evie Kang surprised herself by always being free and this helped spread everyone out enormously. Emily was superb in defence but everyone pulled their weight and it was a big team effort. At times the ball flowed well down the court and the girls won comfortably 6-0. Their footwork is very sound now, their balance and dodging still needs work if they are to be successful at a higher level.

Mrs White
