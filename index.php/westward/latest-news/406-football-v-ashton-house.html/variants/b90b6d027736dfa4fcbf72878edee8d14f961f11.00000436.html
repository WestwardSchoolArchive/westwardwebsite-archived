Football v Ashton House


What a superb afternoon! Despite the long journey, the boys and girls who travelled to The Grasshoppers Rugby Club to take on Ashton House at football and netball had an action packed afternoon.

 

All three age groups played a lengthy set of matches allowing everyone to have a good run. In the U9 match the final score was Ashton House 13 - Westward 11. The Westward goal scorers were Nishaan 8, Connor 2 and Ruaridh 1. Mario had a great run in goal and there was lots of effort from Laurence and Luke. The match was a ding dong affair, with the play swinging to both goals. All the boys gave of their very best and seemed to enjoy themselves. For scoring the most goals, the Player of the Match was Nishaan.
 

The U10 match was a rather one sided affair with Westward winning 8 - 0 in the first game and 7 - 1 in the second. what a good result! The goal scorers in the first game were Leo 4, Eashan 3 and Aayush 1. The second match scorers were Jack 5, Leo 1 and Monty 1. All the boys had a good time and enjoyed their success. Players of the Match were Leo and Jack for their 5 goals apiece.
 

In another one-sided affair, the U11s beat Ashton House 20 - 6 in their two sessions. The goal scorers were Himmat 7, Kristian 6, Tommy 4, Toby 2 and Shayaan 1. Again the boys enjoyed their outing and their success, but had they distributed the ball more, the final score could have been higher. Players of the Match were Himmat and Kristian.

 

All in all, a very successful afternoon out, well worth the traffic jam on the way home. A big thank you to Bruton's Coaches for getting us there and back safely and to the parents who braved Friday traffic to support the boys.

 

Mr Pettipher
