Voice Power Singing Day at Danes Hill School, Tuesday 8 March 2016

 

A selected number of year 4 and year 5 children from Westward School Choir were invited to Danes Hill’s first organised “Voice Power Singing Day”. The theme of the day was “A Disney mashup!”.

 

Ninety eight children from five different schools enjoyed a full day of singing. A  variety of warm up singing exercises at the beginning of the day were followed by two rehearsals familiarising themselves with music, lyrics and actions of the “Disney Medley” which included well known songs such as “A Whole New World” and “The Bare Necessities” .

The highlight of the day was the final production in which the choir and Danes Hill’s music band joined together to deliver a fantastic concert to a spellbound audience.

 

This day was a great music-making opportunity for Westward’s pupils to broaden their musical horizon.
