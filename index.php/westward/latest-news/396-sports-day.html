SPORTS DAY 2016

 

The school’s annual Sports Day took place on the morning of Wednesday 22 June.

 

 

What was meant to be a reasonable day, weather wise, turned into a rather damp affair, with fine rain marring the start of proceedings.

 

However, undaunted by the weather everyone got on with what they had gone to Stompond Lane Athletics Track to do - run, jump and throw. Despite a few slips at the start of the flat races  we managed to get everything done, with some really exciting tussles.

 

The Long  Jump and Ball Throwing produced a few surprises and some good competition. Perhaps the most exciting of the races were the 400m Girls and Boys. The girls saw a very exciting race at the end between Rebecca, Yasmin and Leonie - what a close finish and tremendous sportsmanship after the race. Jamie set the field alight with his run in the 400m, leaving all in his wake.

 

All in all a good day was had by all and there was some very good camaraderie amongst the children which was very pleasing to see.

 

A big thank you to all the parents who came along to support their children, we hope you enjoyed the day.

 

The final results were:

St. Patrick's - 95

St. David's- 96

St. George's - 114

St. Andrew's - 126 - 2016 Champions
