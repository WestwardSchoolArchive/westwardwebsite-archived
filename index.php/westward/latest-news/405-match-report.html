Westward U11 v Danesfield. We won 7-2.


Our first match with the whole of Year 6 present showed great promise for the future.

 

 

Footwork grew with confidence as did the shooting. The constant yelling and shouting from the opposition encouraged our girls to panic and throw wild passes. Eventually I spread everyone out including the opposition and calmed them down sufficiently for the game to flow and there were some very nice backline passes emerging. Eirinn slowed down her shooting and was a great asset in the circle. We were leading nicely by the second quarter and never looked like losing it. A few girls need to practise their footwork at speed as they were tending to take those extra steps. I was heartily encouraged by what I saw and look forward to the next match. Well done girls.

Mrs White
