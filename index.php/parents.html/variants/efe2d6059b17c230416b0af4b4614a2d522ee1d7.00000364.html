PARENTS

 

This section is designed to help parents and prospective parents of the School.

 

Within this section you can download and view important dates, extra curricular timetables and policies.

 

Access to paper copies of all School policies and documents are also available free of charge via the School Manager.

Simply fill in a request form using the 'Contact' drop down menu above and we will email or send you a hard copy of the policy you require. 

 

 

You can also inform the School of any changes to your personal details through the change of address page.
