PUPIL ASSESSMENT

 

At Westward we believe the key purpose of assessment is to move children on in their learning.

 

Assessment is an essential part of the teaching process and regular assessments are made of pupils work to monitor their progress throughout the School year, taking action where necessary.

 

Staff have regular meetings and discussions about pupils’ progress, planning for transition between year groups and ensuring continuity of their academic progress.
