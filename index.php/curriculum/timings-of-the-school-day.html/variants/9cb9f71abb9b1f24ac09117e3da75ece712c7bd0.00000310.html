TIMINGS OF THE SCHOOL DAY

September 2016

 

BREAKFAST CLUB    7.30a.m.- 8.30a.m.

 

Middle Gate opens at 8.30a.m.

Bell rings in the playground at 8.45a.m.

 

EARLY YEARS

NURSERY          

Morning Session: 8.30a.m. - 12p.m.

Lunch: 12p.m. - 1p.m.  

Afternoon Session: 1.00p.m.-3.00p.m.

 

Nursery attend the whole School Assembly every Friday 9.00a.m. – 9.30a.m.

 

RECEPTION

 Morning Session: 8.45a.m. - 12.00p.m.

 Lunch: 12p.m. - 1p.m.  (Thursday lunch 12.30p.m. - 1.30p.m.)

 Afternoon Session: 1p.m. - 3.15p.m.

 

Reception children attend Thursday and Friday Assemblies from 9.00a.m. - 9.30a.m. at the beginning of the Autumn Term, building up to attending all Assemblies by the end of the year.

 

LOWER SCHOOL (Years 1 & 2)  

Morning Session: 8.45a.m. - 12.30p.m.

(There is a 20 minute break during the morning session)

 Lunch: 12.30p.m. - 1.30p.m.

Afternoon Session: 1.30p.m. - 3.20p.m.

(There is a 15 minute break during the afternoon session on certain days)

 

Lower School children attend Assembly on a Monday, Wednesday and Friday from 9.00a.m. - 9.30a.m.

Every Thursday is a Lower School assembly.

 

UPPER SCHOOL (Years 3 to 6)      

Morning Session: 8.45a.m. - 12.30p.m.

(There is a 20 minute break during the morning session)

 Lunch: 12.30p.m. - 1.30p.m.

 Afternoon Session: 1.30p.m. - 3.30p.m.

 

Years 3 and 4 attend Assembly on a Monday, Wednesday and Friday from 9.00a.m. - 9.30a.m.

 

Year 5 attend Assembly on a Wednesday (when no athletics) and Friday from 9.00a.m. - 9.30a.m.

 

Year 6 attend assembly on a Monday (from end of January), Wednesday (when no athletics) and Friday from 9.00a.m. - 9.30a.m.

 

AFTER SCHOOL CARE                   3.00p.m. - 6.00p.m.
