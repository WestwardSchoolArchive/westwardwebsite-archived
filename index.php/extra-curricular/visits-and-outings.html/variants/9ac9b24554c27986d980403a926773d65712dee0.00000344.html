VISITS & OUTINGS

Pupils enjoy a wide variety of trips and visits and are expected to go on at least one outing per term to supplement and enhance the curriculum.

 

Trips include Haslemere Museum, Gunnersbury Park Museum Centre, The Science Museum, London Zoo, Wisley Horticultural Gardens, HMS Victory, The Sutton Life Centre and the Polka Theatre.

 

 

Pupils in Years 3 & 4 go on an annual three day residential trip to New Barn in Dorset.

 

 

 

Pupils in Years 5 & 6 attend a PGL activity weekend in preparation for transfer to senior school.

 


 

Visitors and parents are invited into School such as the fire service, theatre groups, the animal man and the cycling proficiency team to share their knowledge.

 

We also arrange our own special events which include Maths, Literacy, Science and Fire Safety weeks to further enrich the curriculum. In the Spring Term 2016 we will be holding an 'All Around the World' week.
