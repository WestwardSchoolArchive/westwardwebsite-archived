OUT OF SCHOOL CLUBS

 

Breakfast Club

7.30a.m. - 8.30a.m. daily

£5 a session

 

Children can choose from the breakfast menu which includes toast,
cereal, fruit, yogurt and a drink.

 

 

After School Care

3.00p.m. - 6.00p.m. daily

£5 an hour

 

Children choose from the After School menu which includes sandwiches, yoghurt, fruit and a drink of milk, water or fruit squash.

 

Children may also bring their own packed tea if they wish.

 

 

Activities provided within the Out of School Clubs promote play. We also have planned activities of craft, art and cooking.

 

 

Sessions run every day during term time and we are limited to 24 spaces so we ask that parents book any sessions they require on a weekly or termly basis. 

We do also take daily bookings subject to spaces being available.
