ADMISSION AND REGISTRATION

ADMINISTRATION/REGISTRATION FEE:

A non-returnable fee of £100 is required for a child to be placed on the reserved class list.

 

CONFIRMATION FEE:

A confirmation fee of £750 is required to reserve a place at Westward School (Reception – Year 6) following offer

of a place.

This sum is held by the School and returned after the child’s last term (minus any extras accrued) providing

notice is correctly given and acknowledged. A full term's notice in writing is required if a child leaves the

School, otherwise a full term's fees become payable in lieu. Notice will not be accepted after the first day of

term. Notice is not required for those in the final term of Year 6.

If the confirmed place is not taken up, the confirmation fee is not returnable.

 

SCHOOL FEES  -  Payable in full on or before the first day of each term.

Termly accounts are issued from the Bursar via email at the end of the previous term.

*BACS is our preferred method of payment – Bank details can be found on all invoices.

Other methods of payment accepted:

*Cheque (payable to Westward School or Paradigm Educational Limited)

*A School Fee Plan of your choice – please ask our office for further details.

*Childcare Vouchers for Childcare, Please do not set up monthly instalments directly into our account; these

should only be redeemed to pay the appropriate part of termly invoices.

Late payments will incur a minimum charge of £50. A £35 fee will be levied for returned cheques.

 

EARLY YEARS ENTITLEMENT (3 and 4 YEAR OLDS)

Westward is a registered setting with Surrey County Council.

The current maximum Early Years Free Entitlement claim is 15 hours per week. Settings are not expected to

meet the requirements of each individual parent and Surrey County Council is able to provide a list of

alternative settings to suit differing requirements.

For the academic year 2016/17 Westward offers 33 weeks free early entitlement as follows: Autumn 12

weeks, Spring 11 weeks, Summer 10 weeks

All children wishing to take up their free entitlement provision at Westward will be contracted to a minimum

of five morning sessions (08.30-12.00) or three full days (08.30 – 3.00). Any additional “top up” or services will

be charged at the current termly rate.

In the event of Free Early Education Entitlement funding being reclaimed  by Surrey County Council, for any

reason, the reclaimed amount will be payable to the school as an outstanding charge.
