ADMISSIONS PROCEDURE

 

 

Most Friday mornings during term time, 9.45am, prospective parents are welcome to view the school at work. Please inform the school office of your intention to visit beforehand.

 

  • Completion and receipt of the Registration Form with £100.00 (non returnable) registration fee acknowledges initial interest and places your child on the waiting list.

 

  • The Confirmation Fee of £500.00 (KG – Year 6) is payable to reserve an offered place. This payment is returned during the last term providing the correct FULL term’s notice (not accepted after the first day of term) is given.

Please note – if the confirmed place is NOT taken up, the fee is non returnable.

 

  • On receipt of a Confirmation Fee – a school contract will be issued to you for signing.

 

  • Nearer your child’s commencement date parents are given an introductory pack including all necessary documentation and information. A “settling in” session date will be arranged for your child to spend time with the teacher in the class environment.

 

There is not an entry assessment for the Early Years Nursery and Kindergarten classes. For Year 1 upwards, children are invited to spend a school day with their peers for assessment of social and academic ability. (Is it the right school for them?)

 

The assessment day fee is £30.00, non returnable.
