ADMINISTRATION FEE: A non-returnable fee of £100 is required for a child to be placed on the class list.

 

CONFIRMATION FEE: A confirmation fee of £500 is required to reserve a place at Westward (KG – YR6). This sum is held by the school and is returned during his/her last term. If the confirmed place is NOT taken up, the fee is NOT RETURNABLE.

 

SCHOOL FEES are payable on or before the first day of term. Interest will be charged for late payment. A £20.00 charge will be levied for any returned cheques.

 

A full term's notice in writing is required if a child leaves the school, otherwise a full term's fees become payable in lieu. Notice will not be accepted after the first day of term. Notice is not required for those in the final term of Year 6.

 

NURSERY AND KINDERGARTEN CLASSES:

In the event of Free Early Education Entitlement funding being reclaimed by Surrey County Council, for any reason, the reclaimed amount will be payable to the school as an outstanding charge.
