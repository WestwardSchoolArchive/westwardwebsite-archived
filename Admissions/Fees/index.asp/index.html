ADMISSION AND REGISTRATION

ADMINISTRATION/REGISTRATION FEE:

A non-returnable fee of £100 is required for a child to be placed on the reserved class list.

PUPIL DEPOSIT FEE:

The Pupil Deposit Fee of £2000.00 (Reception – Year 6) is payable to reserve an offered place. £1250 of this sum is credited to the child's second invoice. The remaining £750 (minus any accrued extras) is returned after the child's final term providing the correct FULL term’s notice is given.

A full term's notice in writing is required if a child leaves the school, otherwise a full term's fees become payable in lieu. Notice will not be accepted after the first day of term. Notice is not required for those in the final term of Year 6.

If the confirmed place is not taken up, the acceptance fee is not returnable.

SCHOOL FEES  -  Payable in full on or before the first day of each term.

Termly accounts are issued from the Bursar via email at the end of the previous term.

*BACS is our preferred method of payment – Bank details can be found on all invoices.

Other methods of payment accepted:

*Cheque (payable to Paradigm Educational Limited)

*A School Fee Plan of your choice – please ask our office for further details.

*Childcare Vouchers for Childcare, Please do not set up monthly instalments directly into our account; these should only be redeemed to pay the appropriate part of termly invoices.

Late payments will incur a minimum charge of £50. A £35 fee will be levied for returned cheques.

EARLY YEARS ENTITLEMENT (4 YEAR OLDS)

Westward is a registered setting with Surrey County Council.

For the academic year 2021/22 Westward offers 34 weeks UNIVERSAL Entitlement (15hrs) as follows: Autumn 12 weeks, Spring 10 weeks, Summer 12 weeks. 

The school office provide termly costs for your advance financial planning on request.

In the event of Free Early Education Entitlement funding being reclaimed  by Surrey County Council, for any reason, the reclaimed amount will be payable by families to the school as an outstanding charge.

 

 REGISTRATION

Westward School - Reception to Year 6

Register your interest via email admissions@westwardschool.co.uk for a school place reservation.

The non-returnable Registration/Administration Fee will be payable for your child’s name to be placed on a class waiting list.

Following offer of a place available, this may be reserved for your child on receipt of the current Pupil Deposit fee.

This fee must be received by the date stipulated by the School Office, normally in October prior to the subsequent September start

If the confirmed place is not taken up, the fee is not returnable

The school office will assist with any further details required
