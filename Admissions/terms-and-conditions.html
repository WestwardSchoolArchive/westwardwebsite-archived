TERMS AND CONDITIONS

 

REGISTRATION FEE: 

Completion and receipt of the Registration Form and current registration fee (non returnable) places your child on the class list for entry to the school.

A waiting list procedure operates dependent on demand.

 

CONFIRMATION FEE: 

The current confirmation fee is payable to reserve an offered place. This payment is returned during the pupil’s final term, less any outstanding charges, providing the correct FULL TERM’S written notice is given. If notice is given after the first day of term, a further full term’s fees become payable in lieu. Notice is not required for those in the final term of Year 6. If the confirmed place is NOT taken up the fee is non returnable. On receipt of the confirmation fee a school contract will be issued, to be signed by parent or guardian and returned to the school office.

 

TERMLY FEES:

School fees are payable to the School Manager on or before the first day of term. Interest will be charged for late payment. A £20.00 charge will be levied for any returned cheques. Payment may be made by bank transfer, cheque, cash, childcare vouchers or an individual monthly payment scheme via a school fee plan of your choice.

We reserve the right to exclude your child if fees remain unpaid.

 

FREE EARLY EDUCATION ENTITLEMENT PROVISION FOR 3 AND 4 YEAR OLDS: (NURSERY AND KINDERGARTEN)

Westward School is a registered setting with Surrey County Council. All children wishing to take up their free provision in the Nursery will be contracted to a minimum of five morning sessions or three full days. “Additional services” will be charged at the current termly rate. Parents are reminded that the school’s participation in the free entitlement provision is subject to change.

In the event of Free Early Education Entitlement funding being reclaimed by Surrey County Council, for any reason, the reclaimed amount will be payable to the school as an outstanding charge.

 

EXTRAS:

Any extra curricular activities will be invoiced accordingly. Notice of half a term is required for withdrawal. Class workshops, outings, music lessons etc cannot be refunded in the event of your child’s absence through holidays, illness etc.

 

INSURANCE: 

Your child is covered by the school’s liability insurance and KG to Year 6 by the Pupils’ Personal Accident Insurance Scheme (Marsh Ltd).

 

DOCUMENTATION REQUIRED BY SCHOOL OFFICE:

*Copy of birth certificate or passport

*Signed Home-School Agreement

*Signed CONFIDENTIAL/HEALTH/DIETARY/EMERGENCY information

*Signed consent forms for photography, staff portfolios, web site, advertising etc

*Absence/holiday requests

*Medication to be administered

*Change of address or contact numbers

*Preferable email address for various communications i.e. class notices, newsletters etc

 

HOME – SCHOOL AGREEMENT:

This document details the responsibilities expected from the school, the pupil and the pupil’s family and is signed on a termly basis. Copies may be viewed on the web site or the school office.

 

SPECIAL EDUCATIONAL NEEDS:

The school does not undertake to diagnose dyslexia or other specific learning difficulties but will contact you if we have any concerns and will suggest possible assessment procedures if you so wish.
